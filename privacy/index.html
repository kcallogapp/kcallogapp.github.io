Privacy Policy

kcallog is a calorie tracking app for Android. All data you enter — including calorie entries, labels, weight, and settings — is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. Nothing leaves your phone.

kcallog does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no third-party SDKs, no ads, and no internet connection required.

If you uninstall the app, all data is deleted from your device.

If you have questions about this policy, contact kcallogapp@gmail.com.
